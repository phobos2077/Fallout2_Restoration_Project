1:Arroyo's elder lived for many years after the destruction of the Enclave.
2:She was pleased that the ancient separation between Vault 13 and the Vault Dweller had been reconciled, and many were the times she told you that she wished the Vault Dweller were alive to have seen the reconciliation take place.
3:The Elder passed away in her sleep, certain that the safety of your new village had been secured and was now flourishing.
4:Many of the older Arroyo residents believe that she now lives in the vault of the sky, telling the Vault Dwellers of your brave deeds.
